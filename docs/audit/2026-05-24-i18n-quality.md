## 5. i18n architecture notes

- **Append-only protocol** holding strong — no in-place edits detected across the 14-day Phase 4.5 sweep. Pre-commit hook clearly working.
- **`status` namespace bloat** (~95 keys) is starting to act as a catch-all. Consider splitting into `status.empty`/`status.hint` sub-namespaces in a future refactor (would break append-only — needs a dedicated PR).
- **Local dynamic helpers** scattered across `panel` components (`tUnlockedRatio` in `achievements-panel`, `periodLabel` in `stats-panel` before this PR, `sectionLabel` in `template-editor`). Pattern is "kept inline as a local helper rather than added to `src/i18n/dynamic.ts`" — fine for one-off helpers but the recurring `getLocale() === 'en' ? ... : ...` shape suggests adding a `tCond(zh, en)` utility could remove a class of bugs (forgetting one branch). Defer to architecture proposal.
- **DB enum boundary** is clean: `equipment` / `muscleGroup` / `loadType` namespaces flow exclusively through their `tX()` helpers; CHECK constraint values stay zh-only in the schema (per ADR-0019). The `app/exercise/new.tsx::equipment: '其他'` literal is the canonical example — correctly NOT user-facing.
- **`page.exerciseList` vs `page.exerciseListSection`** — duplicate-value key pair likely from a copy-paste during sweep. Future sweep should consolidate or remove the older entry.
